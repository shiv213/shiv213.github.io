Jasoos (જાસૂસ, "imposter") is a Gujarati-language take on Imposter: every player gets a shared word except the Jasoos, who has to bluff their way through the round without getting called out as the imposter. Built as an Expo iOS app sharing the same game logic.

### [Download on the App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/jasoos-party-game/id6757278788)
